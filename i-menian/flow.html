How to Apply for Lab Access

A simple guide — from submitting your form to getting access

📬 Email notifications may land in your Spam / Junk folder — please check there too

📋
You
(Applicant)
Submit Your Application
Fill in the online form with your personal details, choose your lab, category, and supervisor. Hit submit.
You will receive
Email Notification
✅ Application Received
Your form has been submitted and sent to the lab officer for review.
👨‍💼
Lab Officer
(PIC)
Lab Officer Reviews Your Form
The lab officer gets an email, logs in, and checks your application.
❌ Not approved? You'll get an email explaining why. The process ends here.
If approved
Email Notification
👍 Lab Officer Approved — One More Step
Approved by the lab officer. Now waiting for the Head of Lab to confirm.
🏛️
Head of
Lab
Head of Lab Gives Final Approval
The Head of Lab reviews and gives the final go-ahead.
❌ Not approved? You'll get an email explaining why. The process ends here.
If approved — what happens next depends on who you are
👷 Staff
No test needed
Skips straight to final registration.
Email Notification
Approved — Sent to IMEN Officer
🎓 Student / Others
Safety test required
You'll receive a link to take the online safety test.
Email Notification
Please Complete Your Safety Test
🧪
Safety
Test
Complete the Online Safety Test
Click the link in your email and answer all the lab safety questions. You need a perfect score to move forward. Don't worry — you can retake it if needed.
💡 If you have applied to a different lab before, the system will recognise you automatically — no extra steps needed.
Once you pass
Email Notification
🎓 Safety Test Passed — Official Record
Your score and answers are saved as your official lab safety record. Keep this email for your reference.
⏳ After passing, the IMEN Officer will be notified. Sit tight — you'll receive another email soon with your next step.
🔐
IMEN
Officer
IMEN Officer Completes Your Registration
The officer reviews your approved application and activates your lab access.

🎉
All Done
Welcome — Your Lab Access is Active!
You are now registered. Your lab access has been granted. Welcome aboard!
