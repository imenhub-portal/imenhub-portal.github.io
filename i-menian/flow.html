i-Menian System

How to Apply for Lab Access

Follow these steps to get your lab access approved — from form to final confirmation.

📬 Emails may land in your Spam / Junk folder — always check there too
📋
You

Submit Your Application

Fill in the online form with your details — name, student ID, email, lab, category, and supervisor. Then hit submit.

Email notifications sent
📨
Email Notification
Application Received
Your form has been submitted and sent to the lab officer for review.
📨
To: Lab Officer
New Application to Review
Lab officer is notified with your details and a link to approve.
📨
To: Supervisor
For Your Information
Supervisor is informed. No action needed from them.
👨‍💼
Lab Officer (PIC)

Lab Officer Reviews Your Application

The lab officer receives the email, logs in to i-Menian, and checks your application details.

✕
Not approved? You will receive an email with the reason. The process stops here.
✓
If approved — you will receive an email notifying you of the next step.
📨
Email Notification
Lab Officer Approved — One More Step to Go
Now waiting for the Head of Lab to give the final confirmation.
🏛️
Head of Lab

Head of Lab Gives Final Approval

The Head of Lab reviews the application and gives the final institutional go-ahead.

✕
Not approved? You will receive an email with the reason. The process stops here.
✓
If approved — what happens next depends on your category.
👷 Staff
No test required
Skips straight to final registration with the IMEN Officer.
Email Notification
Approved — Sent to IMEN Officer
🎓 Student / Others
Safety test required
You will receive a link to complete the online safety test.
Email Notification
Please Complete Your Safety Test
🧪
Safety Test

Complete the Online Safety Test

Click the link in your email and answer all lab safety questions. A perfect score is required to move forward. You may retake if needed.

💡
If you have applied for a different lab before, the system will recognise you automatically — no extra steps needed.
Once you pass
📨
Email Notification
Safety Test Passed — Official Record
Your score and answers are saved as your official lab safety record. Keep this email.
⏳
The IMEN Officer will be notified. Sit tight — you will receive another email shortly with your next step.
🔐
IMEN Officer
